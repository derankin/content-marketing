# Founder Short-Form Video Talk Tracks (30–45s)

## Video 1 — “Where destination wedding stress really comes from”
**Hook:** "Quick founder insight: destination wedding stress usually isn’t the venue—it’s guest coordination."
**Talking points:**
- Info gets fragmented across too many channels
- Couples lose time chasing responses
- Centralized workflows reduce anxiety fast
**CTA:** "If you’re planning one, simplify guest ops early."

## Video 2 — “Premium = reliable operations”
**Hook:** "Hot take: premium wedding experiences are mostly an operations decision."
**Talking points:**
- Beautiful design matters, but reliability builds trust
- RSVP flows must handle real complexity
- Operational clarity is the real luxury
**CTA:** "Want the checklist we use for RSVP flow quality?"

## Video 3 — “Build for behavior, not assumptions”
**Hook:** "Guests won’t all use one channel. Your workflow should reflect that."
**Talking points:**
- Real behavior spans SMS, WhatsApp, email, calls
- Single-channel assumptions create follow-up fatigue
- One source of truth + multi-channel comms is the unlock
**CTA:** "Founders: where are assumptions creating friction in your product?"
