# Founder LinkedIn Voice — Dennis Rankin

## Objective
Build trust and authority by posting as a founder-operator: honest lessons, tactical insights, and product conviction.

## Voice Profile
- Clear, direct, no fluff
- Builder energy: "here’s what we learned shipping"
- Customer-first, not hype-first
- Warm and human, but grounded in execution

## Post Formula (default)
1. **Hook:** contrarian or specific observation
2. **Story:** what happened in the business
3. **Lesson:** what changed in your thinking
4. **Action:** what you’re doing next
5. **CTA:** invite conversation, not hard sell

## Content Buckets
1. Building Addressed in public (wins + mistakes)
2. Destination wedding operations insights
3. Product decisions (why we built X this way)
4. Founder systems (speed, focus, shipping)
5. Customer signal (what couples/planners actually need)

## Do / Don’t
### Do
- Use first person singular (I) for founder insights
- Share specifics (workflow, tradeoff, decision)
- End with a question to spark thoughtful comments

### Don’t
- Overclaim growth/performance
- Generic motivation quotes with no operational takeaway
- Overly polished corporate tone
