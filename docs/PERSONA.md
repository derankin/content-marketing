# Persona: Addressed Audience Profiles

## Primary Audience 1: DIY Destination Wedding Couples

### Who they are
Couples planning a destination wedding without a full-service planner, often balancing romance with real-world logistics.

### Motivations
- Create a meaningful wedding experience that feels personal, stylish, and memorable
- Keep planning stress low while coordinating guests across locations
- Use modern tools instead of endless spreadsheets and text threads

### Pain points
- Chasing guests for mailing addresses and RSVP responses
- Managing plus-ones, sub-events, travel details, and dietary needs in one place
- Communicating clearly with guests across SMS, email, and WhatsApp

### What they want from Addressed
- A calm, modern planning experience
- Better guest communication with less manual follow-up
- Confidence that nothing falls through the cracks before travel

---

## Primary Audience 2: Wedding Planners (Destination-focused)

### Who they are
Independent planners and boutique planning teams managing multiple destination events and high-touch client expectations.

### Motivations
- Deliver smooth guest experiences at scale
- Save team time on repetitive outreach and RSVP tracking
- Present a modern, premium process to clients

### Pain points
- Fragmented systems for invites, guest lists, RSVP updates, and communications
- Last-minute guest changes and missed responses
- Manual follow-ups that consume billable time

### What they want from Addressed
- One operational system for invitations + guest management
- Cleaner workflows for communication and RSVP handling
- A polished planning stack that elevates client trust

---

## Shared Psychographic: “Lover-Traveler”

The emotional center of the brand audience is couples (and planners serving them) who see weddings as an experience, not just an event.

### Identity statement
"Our love language is: let’s book a trip."

### Brand resonance cues
- Adventure with intention
- Romance with logistics handled elegantly
- Modern, tasteful, globally aware
- Aspirational but never out-of-touch

## Messaging Implication
Addressed should sound like a trusted travel-savvy host: warm, organized, and effortlessly on top of details.
