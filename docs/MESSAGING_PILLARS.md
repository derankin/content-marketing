# Messaging Pillars: Addressed

## Positioning Statement
Addressed is the modern destination wedding guest management platform for couples and planners who want a beautiful guest experience without the operational chaos.

## Pillar 1: Romance, Not Spreadsheet Stress
**Core message:** The wedding should feel magical; planning should feel manageable.
- Emotional hook: "Your love story deserves more than tabs and text chains."
- Utility proof: central guest list, RSVP tracking, and communication workflows
- Best for: DIY couples, awareness-stage content

## Pillar 2: Built for Destination Complexity
**Core message:** Destination weddings have more moving parts; Addressed keeps everyone aligned.
- Emotional hook: "When your wedding includes passports and plus-ones, details matter."
- Utility proof: sub-events, custom RSVP logic, guest updates across channels
- Best for: consideration-stage and planner content

## Pillar 3: Communication That Guests Actually Respond To
**Core message:** Meet guests where they are (SMS/WhatsApp/email) and reduce follow-up fatigue.
- Emotional hook: "Less chasing. More celebrating."
- Utility proof: SMS-first address collection, AI concierge support, WhatsApp compatibility
- Best for: conversion-stage content

## Pillar 4: Premium Experience, Practical Workflow
**Core message:** Deliver a polished, modern planning experience without adding overhead.
- Emotional hook: "Luxury feel, operational calm."
- Utility proof: cleaner workflows for couples and planners, fewer misses and last-minute scrambles
- Best for: planner audience and founder POV

## Brand Promise (Safe)
Addressed helps destination couples and planners coordinate guest details with more clarity and less manual effort.

## Proof Themes to Repeat
- Time reclaimed from manual follow-up
- Fewer missed guest details
- Cleaner communication experience for everyone
- More confidence before travel

## CTA Framework
- Awareness: "See how modern destination couples manage guests."
- Consideration: "Explore a smoother RSVP workflow."
- Conversion: "Start your destination guest list the modern way."
