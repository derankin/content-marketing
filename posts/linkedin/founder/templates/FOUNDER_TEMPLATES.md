# 10 Founder LinkedIn Post Templates (Dennis)

## 1) The Pattern I Keep Seeing
**Hook:** "The pattern I keep seeing in [industry] is [observation]."
**Body:** What you noticed building Addressed + why it matters.
**Close:** "Are you seeing this too?"

## 2) We Were Wrong About X
**Hook:** "I was wrong about [assumption]."
**Body:** Original belief → what customer reality taught you.
**Close:** "What assumption did your market break for you?"

## 3) The Tradeoff Post
**Hook:** "You can optimize for [A] or [B]—rarely both at once."
**Body:** Product/ops tradeoff and decision logic.
**Close:** "How do you decide this in your team?"

## 4) Build-in-Public Lesson
**Hook:** "Shipping this week taught me one thing: [lesson]."
**Body:** Small shipped change + downstream impact.
**Close:** "Want a breakdown of the process?"

## 5) Customer Signal > Opinion
**Hook:** "The loudest opinion wasn’t the best signal."
**Body:** How real user behavior shaped roadmap.
**Close:** "What signal do you trust most?"

## 6) Founder System That Actually Helps
**Hook:** "A system I use to avoid founder thrash:"
**Body:** 3-5 bullets of workflow/process.
**Close:** "Happy to share the template."

## 7) Behind One Product Decision
**Hook:** "Why we built [feature/workflow] this way."
**Body:** Context, constraints, outcome expected.
**Close:** "Would you have made the same call?"

## 8) Myth vs Reality in Wedding Tech
**Hook:** "Myth: [common belief]. Reality: [truth]."
**Body:** Explain with practical example.
**Close:** "What myths are still costing teams time?"

## 9) Operator POV on Premium Experience
**Hook:** "Premium is mostly an operations decision."
**Body:** Connect brand feel to process reliability.
**Close:** "Where does your process shape perception?"

## 10) Weekly Founder Recap
**Hook:** "This week building Addressed:"
**Body:** 3 wins, 1 challenge, 1 focus next week.
**Close:** "If you’re building in events/weddings, let’s compare notes."
