I used to think wedding planning friction was mostly "organizational."
Now I think it’s mostly "channel mismatch."

Guests live in text messages, WhatsApp, email, and calls.
Most planning workflows still assume one channel and perfect behavior.

That gap creates follow-up fatigue.

A big part of our work at Addressed is meeting guests where they already are — while giving couples/planners one source of truth.

It sounds obvious, but it changes everything operationally.

If you run customer comms in your startup: are you designing around your system, or user behavior?
