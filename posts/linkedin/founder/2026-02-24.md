One founder lesson I’m learning in wedding tech:
"Beautiful" and "useful" can’t be separate teams.

For destination weddings, the experience has to feel premium *and* run reliably.

If the brand feels aspirational but RSVP workflows break under real-world complexity (plus-ones, sub-events, dietary notes), trust disappears.

At Addressed, we’re treating guest operations like product infrastructure:
- reduce manual follow-up
- centralize communication
- keep every guest touchpoint clear

A premium experience is ultimately an operations decision.

Founders: where have you seen "design vs utility" tensions show up in your product?
